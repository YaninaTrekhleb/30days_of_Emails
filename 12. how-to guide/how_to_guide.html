How to Set Up Your SmartHome WiFi Light Bulbs

Follow our simple steps to get started.

Step 1: Install the Bulb

Screw the SmartHome WiFi bulb into any standard light socket. Ensure the switch is turned off during installation for safety.

Step 2: Download the App

Download the SmartHome app from your device's app store. Create an account and follow the on-screen instructions to pair your bulbs.

Step 3: Connect to WiFi

Follow the prompts in the app to connect the bulbs to your home's WiFi network. Make sure your WiFi is on and the signal is strong.
